Frequently Asked Questions

What is ethical hacking?

Ethical hacking involves authorized attempts to breach systems to find vulnerabilities.

How do I know if I need penetration testing?

If you have sensitive data, operate in a regulated industry, or want to ensure your security posture, you should consider it.

What types of reports do you provide?

We provide comprehensive reports detailing findings, risks, and recommendations for remediation.

How long does a penetration test take?

The duration varies based on the scope, but typically takes 1-2 weeks.

Do you provide post-test support?

Yes, we offer support and guidance for remediation after the testing is completed.
